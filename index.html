Visit Auroville

Auroville wants to be a universal town where men and women of all countries are able to live in peace and progressive harmony above all creeds, politics and nationalities.

Places to visit in Auroville

Matrimandir

Visitors and guests should bear in mind that the Matrimandir is not a ‘tourist’ site; it is a place for individual silent concentration. A place which should be visited in a quiet physical and mental state.

Auroville Beach

Auroville Beach is located in Pondicherry. It is a part of the Auroville Ashram and is situated about 10 kilometres away. A perfect spot for Sunday evening.

Adishakti Theatre

Adishakti Laboratory for Theatre Arts Research is a performing arts company which also provides stay and accommodation facilities Located on the outskirts of Pondicherry, the theatre is known for its brilliant and evocative performances.
